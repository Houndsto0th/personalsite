What Makes Me Tick?

I’ve been straddling the line between a very relaxed “surf” mentality and a highly competitive nature since I was small. I enjoy a fast paced environment where people depend on each other to do their work well and to foster continued learning and exploration.

I love having a hand in both construction and design and I want to find a place that allows me to get incredible at both sides of the “developer/designer” coin.


Background

My background is primarily in retail with two notable exceptions (Army and Apple in Cupertino). In the past couple of years I started to develop an interest in web/applications and design. I studied “Digital Design” at CU Denver when the program was still in it’s fledgling state.

After hearing about Galvanize and gSchool I found a way to make it happen and now I’m happily spending my days moving divs and troubleshooting performance hiccups in API requests.
